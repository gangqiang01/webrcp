Bullish about the market prospects of the mobile industry, Inventec Corporation and Advantech Co., Ltd formed AIMobile Co., Ltd to focus on the research and development of industrial handheld wireless devices, its production and marketing, its product application areas including retail, automotive, medical and other vertical markets. AIMobile will provide customers with the highest standards of industrial computer tablets and other handheld devices, design and manufacturing services with a special emphasis on industrial application of Android operating system and its peripheral hardware and software support for the development of a complete solution.

Through this strategic alliance between Inventec and Advantech, AIMobile utilizes the resources of both companies to strive to achieve its objectives of designing and manufacturing handheld mobile devices for the global industry. With Advantech's understanding of the market and of the customers' needs and Inventec's R&D investment resources in handheld mobile products' hardware and software, AIMobile is able to provide customers with the highest standards industrial computer tablets and other handheld devices, design and manufacturing services.
